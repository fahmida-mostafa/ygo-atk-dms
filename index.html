Yu-Gi-Oh! Attack - Duel Monsters

Instructions

  1. Select a monster from your side.
  2. Select a monster from your opponent's side.
  3. Attack.

To de-select cards at any time, click on cancel.

Your opponent (AI) then needs to complete their turn before you can attack again.

Whoever gets the other player's life points down to zero wins!
